Buy One, Nourish Two.

The Hope Bar was designed to provide BOTH the consumer AND the children we nourish with standardized whole food nutritional support.

You Need It

Your body is designed to crave food until it is properly nourished. But in our modern diets we seldom meet those needs, so the cravings never stop.
It’s called the obesity paradox: over-fed but undernourished.

We Provide It In Our Products

The Hope Bar is the first food bar packed with whole food-sourced vitamins, minerals, and advanced immune support to help fill those nutritional gaps.
No Synthetics • Non GMO • Gluten Free.

They Also Receive It

Your decision to nourish yourself with the Hope Bar allows us to send whole food nutritional support to children in need in the United States and throughout the world.

Why We Give

When children are malnourished, their development is compromised. This often results in stunted growth, diminished brain and cognitive functions and a weakened immune system, so they are more susceptible to chronic infections and disease.


Malnutrition is the condition that develops when the body does not get the right amount of the vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients it needs to maintain healthy tissues and organ function.

What We Give

Hope Blend

Hope Blend provides 100% of the micronutrient vitamins and minerals and advanced immune support from whole food sources that a child needs for an entire day; Putting Hope Blend into a child’s food turns whatever they eat into the most nourishing thing they will ever eat.

Why We’re Different


The Hope Bar Project was designed to provide BOTH the consumer AND the children we nourish with standardized whole food nutritional support. New Social businesses have come into the market over the last few years that are connecting the sale of their food products (typically snack bars) to the donation of a fortified food packet to bring relief to an undernourished child somewhere in the world. While this is a noble effort, it falls short in two important areas. First, in today’s world, both the consumer as well as the child have a critical need for good nutrition. And second, the human body was designed to utilize nutrients from food, not from synthetically made vitamins and minerals. Thats why our Project offers real “Hope” to everyone involved.
