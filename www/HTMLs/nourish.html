Why We’re Different


The Hope 2 4 1 initiative was designed to provide BOTH the consumer AND the children we nourish with standardized whole food nutritional support. New Social businesses have come into the market over the last few years that are connecting the sale of their food products (typically snack bars) to the donation of a fortified food packet to bring relief to an undernourished child somewhere in the world. While this is a noble effort, it falls short in two important areas. First, in today’s world, both the consumer as well as the child have a critical need for whole food nutritional support, not just another sugar filled, nutrient deficient snack food. And second, the human body was designed to best utilize nutrients from food, not from synthetically made vitamins and minerals. That’s why our Initiative offers real “Hope” to everyone involved.
