HOPE “FAT BURNER” PROGRAM

Reset Your Metabolism

And turn your body into the healthy fat-burning machine it was designed to be!

Resetting your metabolism is about making food choices that help support the body’s normal fat-burning, muscle-building functions. We were designed to burn fat as our primary source of fuel, but our modern diets contain foods and ingredients that have dramatically compromised those functions.

Fat loss has more to do with hormones than with calorie-counting. Hormones regulate virtually every aspect of your body’s metabolic pathways. They can cause you to either store fat or burn fat and to build or tear down muscle. Hormones can make you feel full or hungry, and they handle about a million other functions that factor into your weight, fitness, and overall well-being.

Constant over-consumption of sugar and sugar producing carbs combined with a lack of healthy fats in our diets can throw these critical hormones completely out of balance. This imbalance forces your body to burn sugar, instead of fat, as its primary source of fuel and to convert all the excess sugar into fat. To convert your body from a sugar burner to a fat burner requires the rebooting and re-balancing of your hormones. Traditional “diet plans” and “diet foods” do not support this shift and can only provide temporary weight loss results. That’s why most people that lose weight on a diet, gain  back what they lost and then some. And it typically comes back with a vengeance.

The Hope “Fat Burner” Program was developed to help shift your metabolism back to its efficient fat burning state.  The plan  is divided into 2 Phases; the initial 15 day “RESET” Phase and the ongoing “IMPACT” Phase. In both phases, the Hope “Reset” Bar can help you meet your nutritional needs and weight loss goals.

The Reset Phase is designed to help REBOOT your hormones, RESTORE your digestive tract, REACTIVATE your immune system, and REPLENISH your cells in order to shift your metabolism. There are specific foods to eat and not to eat to best accomplish this goal. The Impact Phase comes after the Reset Phase. This Phase is designed to help perpetuate your new fat burning lifestyle. The Impact Phase expands the list of foods you can eat and even includes mandatory “vacation” meals.  Vacation meals allow you to eat any food you want or crave and serve the critical function of helping to reboot your fat burning hormones on a consistent basis (your body’s production of leptin can drop up to 50 Percent when overall caloric intake is reduced). Vacation meals also help you overcome the perception of having to give up your favorite foods. You can still have your favorite ice cream or pizza, just at intervals that get your fat burning hormones to work for you rather than against you.

Reset Phase

15 Day Reset Phase Meal Program

Simply:

  • Pick a protein
  • Pick a healthy fat
  • Pick your fruits and/or your vegetables

Click here for printable list of Reset Phase Food Choices

*Approved fruits and vegetables can be eaten at any meal or as a snack

There are 5 food rules during the “Reset” phase (15 Days)

  1. Eat 2 or more Hope “Reset” Bars per day during the Reset Phase.
  2. Don’t Skip Meals
  3. Fat: Increase your intake of healthy fats
  4. Protein: Moderate your intake – on average 4-6 ounces per meal (Too much protein will also convert to sugar)
  5. Carbohydrates: Eliminate grains, sugars, and most fruits (see approved list below)

*Make sure you drink a 16 oz Bottle of Water with each Bar

After you’ve completed your 15 day Reset Phase, you can continue to adhere to the Reset Food Program or you can shift into the broader Impact Phase. But, with either choice, you should now incorporate vacation meals into your weekly eating program.

“Vacation” Meal Guidelines

  • Eat 2 Vacation Meals per week. (It often works best to combine them into the same day.)
  • Eat the foods you crave: This is not a cheat meal, so don’t feel guilty for eating the foods you really want.
  • Don’t overdo it; eating to the point of misery is not the purpose
  • Don’t skip a meal in order to prepare yourself for your vacation meal
  • Don’t keep left-overs. They’ll just tempt you in the days that follow.
  • Have Fun- This is your vacation

Impact Phase

Impact Meal Phase:

  • Pick a Protein (Same List as Reset)
  • Pick a Fat (Same List as Reset)
  • Pick your approved fruits and vegetables (Same List as Reset)
  • Continue to eat Hope Reset Bars
  • Optional: Once your weight loss goals are achieved, you can now add higher glycemic fruits, vegetables, or grains to any meal.
